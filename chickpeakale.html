Chickpea Kale Sweet Potato Bowl

Description


This is my favorite food EVER. Roasted chickpeas with steamed kale and sweet potat magic. Mmmmmmmmmm


Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 half sweet potato
  • 1 T vegetable stock
  • 1 T hemp seeds and pumpkin seeds (each)
  • Tahini sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the chickpeas in vegetable stock water until all evaporated
  2. Steam the kale on top of that process
  3. Bake the sweet potato in the oven at 400 degrees F
  4. Cut sweet potato into large chunks
  5. Make tahini sauce (tahini, lemon juice, oat milk, salk, garlic powder, salt)
  6. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and feast
