Cucumber Salad

Description


Cucumber salad is extremely delicious and quite healthy. It is best in the Summer when temperatures are hot and you don't want to cook. You can make it in 10 minutes and have lots of leftovers for the future lazy days. Cucumber salad is a gift from the heavens.


Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Dill
  • White vinegar
  • Maple syrup
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Dice onion into slivers
  2. Add onions to bowl with 5 T white vinegar, 1 t maple syrup, and fill the rest with water
  3. Microwave 90 seconds, then let sit with plate on top
  4. Chop cucumbers and tomatoes into smallish chunks
  5. Pour out vinegar water from onions
  6. Combine all ingredients together with some chopped dill and salt
