Brocoli Rice Bowl

Description


Brocoli and rice! One of my favorite foods since hte beginning of time


Ingredients

  • Brocoli
  • Rice
  • Ginger sauce
  • Tofu
  • Hemp seeds

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Steam brocoli
  3. Bake tofu in soy sauce (or cook on stovetop)
  4. Add everything to bowl and cover with ginger sauce and hemp seeds
